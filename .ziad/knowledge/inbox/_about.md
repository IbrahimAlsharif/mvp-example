# Knowledge — inbox/

Raw, unvalidated input. Files here have not yet been confirmed by a primary
source or cross-checked against existing knowledge.

## What belongs here

- Research notes from a single session before validation
- Analyst feedback that has not been cross-validated
- Draft observations from a product review
- Output from `/ziad-refresh`, `/ziad-learn`, and domain update skills
- Competitive findings pending deeper review

## When to promote

Promote to `domain/` or `project/` when:
- The insight is confirmed by a primary source
- It has been cross-validated against existing knowledge
- It produces a durable change in how Ziad reviews this domain

Delete from inbox once promoted or superseded.

## File naming

`<type>-<YYYY-MM-DD>[b,c,...].md` — e.g., `refresh-2026-04-30.md`,
`refresh-2026-04-30b.md` (for multiple runs on the same day)

## Currency

Inbox files have short `review_after` windows (1–7 days). Stale inbox files
that were never promoted are candidates for deletion, not promotion.
