# Competitor Index — Digital Legacy & Memory Preservation (VERIFIED)

> **Verification status:** This pass fetched vendor sites + official app-store
> listings directly. Features/pricing tagged `official` are read from a primary
> source; traction marked **UNVERIFIED** could not be confirmed (Google Play
> detail pages and some sites block programmatic fetch). This **supersedes** the
> earlier scan, which missed the scaled/funded players and listed a defunct one.
>
> **Market read (unchanged, now evidenced): fragmented.** Each rival owns ONE of
> HTN's four core values; none unify Preserve + Navigate + Share-with-trust +
> Legacy. But two corrections to the earlier read (see Conclusions): the
> recurring family-memory **habit is NOT white space** (Tinybeans/FamilyAlbum own
> it at scale), and the legacy/estate segment is **better funded** than assumed
> (Empathy ~$162M).

## By Core Value

### Navigate — life-timeline browsing & playback (most crowded; thinnest traction)
- **Lifely — My Life / Timeline Maker Journal** — `official`. Closest single-feature
  match: zoomable/scrollable timeline, time-hop, parallel stories, per-event version
  history, claims encryption. iOS+Android, **$4.99/mo or $29.99/yr**. Traction tiny:
  **4.4★ / only 5 ratings**, updated Jul 2025. Owns Navigate+Preserve; **no Share
  circles, no Legacy.** Still the #1 Navigate threat on feature depth.
- **Timelined** (timelined.app) — `official`. Apple-only (iPhone/iPad/Mac/visionOS),
  rigorous metadata + best-in-cluster export (PDF/MD/CSV). Local-only ("developer
  collects no data") → strong privacy but **no cloud backup** (durability risk).
  Free→$0.99/mo / $24.99 lifetime. **5.0★ / 4 ratings** — brand-new (v1.4.1, Jun 2026).
- **Timelines — Shared Journal** (Google Play, INTROSPECTIVE LTD) — `official` desc;
  **pricing & traction UNVERIFIED** (Play detail blocked). Per-timeline private OR
  shared with invite/comment/co-contribute — closest existing thing to privacy
  circles, but binary (private/shared), not Me-Only/Family/Public tiers.
- **ArchiveME** — `official` concept; **all metrics UNVERIFIED**. Family video-journal
  (SIGMOs/COREMOs), explicitly built so future generations watch a relative's video
  logs after they pass — overlaps Navigate+Legacy. iOS+Android.

### Share with trust — privacy-first family memory (the REAL traction leaders live here)
- **Tinybeans** — `official`. **Category leader. 4.9★ / 104K ratings, 500K+ Play
  installs, "millions of parents."** Invite-only child photo journal + 300+
  milestones. $7.99/mo / $74.99/yr. → see **tinybeans.md**. Privacy is UI-circle
  only; **behavioral data sold for ads** on free tier (media stays private).
- **FamilyAlbum / Mitene** (MIXI) — `official`. **4.9★ / 357K ratings, 10M+ installs,
  free unlimited storage.** "No followers. No strangers." Cleaner ads posture than
  Tinybeans ("we don't sell your data, even anonymized"). → see **family-album.md**.
- **Trustworthy** — `official`. "Family operating system" docs/legacy vault.
  **The only player enforcing privacy at the asset level** (AES-256, MFA, YubiKey,
  SOC 2 T2, irreversible aliasing) + a real after-death legacy-access workflow.
  $15M Series A (~$19.7M total). Web+iOS+Android. → see **trustworthy.md**.
- **FamilyPine** — `official`. Privacy-first "everything in-the-family," but
  **negligible traction (3.0★ / 2 ratings, 100+ installs), solo dev, very early.**
  App-store privacy label discloses PII despite "no data mining" marketing.
- **Hiyalife** — **DEFUNCT.** Site offline (ECONNREFUSED). 2012–13 London startup
  ("Tumblr on a timeline," per-memory privacy); no evidence of current operation.
- **23snaps / Cluster / FamilyAlbum-likes** — `official`. Smaller invite-only private
  albums; encryption/media-URL protection **UNVERIFIED**.

### Legacy across generations — future capsules, posthumous delivery, inheritance (mature + funded)
- **Empathy — LifeVault** — the **funded heavyweight: ~$162M total ($72M Series C
  2025), insurer distribution (Aflac/NY Life/Voya), ~7M people reached in 4 months.**
  Wills, directives, executor, secure legal-doc storage. → see **empathy-lifevault.md**.
- **My Heartspace** — `official`. Schedule text/video/audio to milestones (≤18 yr) or
  after death. **Per-message credit pricing** (€2.99–€29.99). Critical: **NO account
  inheritance** — only pre-composed messages release, the timeline does not transfer.
- **GoodTrust** — `official`. Estate platform (attorney will+trust+vault) + scheduled
  Future Messages. Flat **$149**. AES-256. Web-primary.
- **SafeBeyond** — **CONFIRMED DEFUNCT (2026-06-14)** — ceased operations ~July 2022;
  safebeyond.com + /plans unreachable June 2026 (independently reproduced). 2015 "world's
  first posthumous delivery"; notable design = date+event+**geo** triggers + **2-person
  trustee death verification** (the most concrete human-gate found). Founded 2014 (M. Zur,
  Tel Aviv), ~$1.1M raised, Munich Re distribution — the category's clearest cautionary
  case: funded + insurer-backed, still gone in ~8 yrs. → see posthumous-delivery Refresh.
- **Time Guardian / Athar** — `official`. **Blockchain (Ternoa) + 5-TEE key split** —
  strongest durability/self-custody claim; crypto-seedphrase inheritance; Death/Group-
  Consent protocols. Niche (Web3). **Play 1K+ installs.**
- **Evaheld** — `official`. AU AI-assisted ("Charli") all-in-one vault: memoir +
  advance-care + estate + future messages; Family/Care/Private **Rooms** (closest to
  HTN circles). Free→AUD$9/mo→$399 lifetime. The most "HTN-shaped" legacy product.
- **Platform-native (the free baseline that prices the whole segment):** Apple Digital
  Legacy (legacy contact + death certificate), Google Inactivity Manager, Facebook
  Legacy Contact / memorialization. **Free** → structural pricing pressure.
- **Time-capsule novelty apps** (TimeLock, TimeCapsule.io, etc.) — mostly **scheduled-
  date only, NOT death-aware**; validate the capsule UX, don't solve verification/inheritance.

### Preserve — storytelling & memoir
- **StoryWorth** — `official`. Benchmark. Weekly prompt → printed hardcover.
  **1M books printed since 2013.** Only product with **confirmed free export**.
  No app needed. $59–$199/yr. One-shot book, no living timeline, no posthumous trigger.
- **Klokbox** — `official`. Most timeline-native of this group; 1-click gallery import,
  "Keyholders" unlock at time/condition incl. posthumous. **2 ratings** (negligible).
- **AI-memoir cluster** — LifeHistory (AI "Ella" biographer, timeline-centric,
  print $0.50/pg), LifeScribe AI (AI chapters + voice clone), Miigen (human biographer).
  All `official`; traction minimal (≤5 ratings where visible). Export UNVERIFIED except StoryWorth.

## Analyst conclusions (revised on evidence)
1. **Differentiation = the combination, not any single feature.** CONFIRMED — every
   quadrant has an incumbent; the moat is the unified, decades-scale system. No
   competitor unifies all four values.
2. **Future Capsules will NOT differentiate.** CONFIRMED and strengthened —
   scheduled/posthumous delivery is commoditized (My Heartspace/GoodTrust/Evaheld/Athar)
   AND the platform giants give a baseline away free. Correctly rated 🟠.
3. **Privacy is a wedge — but only at the MEDIA/ACCESS layer, not the circle UI.**
   Sharpened: NO multimedia memory competitor (Tinybeans, FamilyAlbum, FamilyPine,
   Cluster, 23snaps) documents object-level URL/access enforcement — all are UI
   invite-circles. The ONLY player enforcing privacy architecturally is Trustworthy,
   and it's a *documents* vault, not a memory timeline. → directly validates review
   heuristic **H-A1** ("the leak is the URL, not the circle UI"). HTN must beat the
   incumbents on enforcement, which none of them have.
4. **The continuous-habit bet is NOT empty white space — it is contested at scale, in
   a different frame.** CORRECTION to the earlier read: Tinybeans (104K ratings) and
   FamilyAlbum (10M installs) prove a *recurring family-memory habit* exists at scale —
   but framed as **child photo-journaling**, with NO Navigate depth and NO Legacy.
   The genuinely open lane is **recurring capture bound to a navigable, inheritable
   life timeline** — which none of the scaled players attempt.

## Top threats to track (revised)
